About Project

As the technology is growing immensely in recent years, certain improvisations need to be made. Suppose we have too many lines of text data in forms of articles, magazines or on social media, you may have to dig through its content and spend a lot of time weeding out the unnecessary stuff before getting the information you want. Automatic Text Summarization allows to transform lengthy documents into shortened versions, something which could be difficult and costly to undertake if done manually.
